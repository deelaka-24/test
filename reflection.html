Peer Observation

The lesson was conducted in a highly motivating and impressive teaching learning environment. Although the lesson was grounded in Pupils' text book, grade 11 to improve their reading competence, It was appropriately Supported by modern technology, a digital story, practicing tasks and recording. The lesson smoothly flowed within. the given period of time through the well organized stages. present, Practice and Production. The content was very inspiring since the setting of the story was placed in war time era in Hitler's regime. The piece of horrible war was felt by the adolescents' inquisitive, logical and empathetic mindset according to Piaget, the educational Psychologist. Further the text is an eye opener to the adult world, informing the unwavering courage of youth confronting a tragedy The class of students well managed by the teacher personality right through with her appropriate dress code gestures, eye contact, a good rapport and positions to maintain order, individual attention, giving instructions and grouped, paired monitoring activities that are performed in and individual manner. The effort of improving macro and micro skills of reading skills was ultimately and evidently Successful as mentioned in the short, motivating, achievable the time framed objectives. the on-going Assessment process ended up giving a constructive Feedback on performances. The instructor was flexible hence witnessed a smooth flow and minute learners' behavioral changes towards Success. I wish her good luck.
